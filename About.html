The Founders

A Story of
Solving Real Problems

Errand Logix Ltd was founded with a clear mission: to make errands and deliveries effortless for individuals and businesses across Lagos. As life became increasingly fast-paced, the need for a dependable logistics partner became impossible to ignore.

The company was established after observing recurring challenges in Lagos: delayed deliveries, poor distribution of purchased goods, and long waiting periods that slowed everyday activities. Determined to solve this, a smarter, faster, and more reliable platform was built.
