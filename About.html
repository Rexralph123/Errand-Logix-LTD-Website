Our Story

Errand Logix Ltd was founded with a clear mission: to make deliveries and errands effortless for individuals and businesses across Lagos. As life became increasingly fast-paced, the need for a dependable logistics partner became impossible to ignore.

The company was established by Ugonna Michael Obisike, Founder and CEO, after observing recurring challenges in Lagos: delayed deliveries, poor distribution of purchased goods, and long waiting periods that slowed everyday activities. Determined to solve this problem, he set out to build a smarter, faster, and more reliable delivery platform. He later met up with his friend, Aneke Fortune Chidera, and together they began working on building the solution into a structured and scalable company.
